Yellowstone National Park is in the United States, was made in 1872, and is the oldest and largest national park in the USA. The park has a very good view of the mountains, forests, lakes, waterfalls, canyons, hot springs, and 100,000 geysers. It is home to a number of animal species, including red foxes, and has a lot of vegetation with forests of spruce. Forest fires have shaped the land over many years. Yellowstone Lake is surrounded by forest, and it has geysers and hot springs that support a large wildlife. In Yellowstone, you will find the cool hot springs with special colors due to mineral deposits in the water despite the high temperatures. The national park is part of a unique ecosystem, which has been essential for the conservation of bison, bears, and wolves.
